Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to request account deletion and what happens to your data.

How to request

  1. Send an email to support@carmonaos.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. We'll verify your identity and confirm receipt within 2 business days.
  4. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.

What happens to your data

Data Type Action
Email address & profile Deleted
Workout history & session logs Deleted
Preferences & settings Deleted
AI debrief & coaching data Deleted
Payment & billing records Retained 7 yrs*

* Payment records are retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations. These records are managed by Stripe and do not include full payment card details.

Important

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All workout data, progress history, and personalized programming will be lost. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it before requesting deletion to avoid further charges.
